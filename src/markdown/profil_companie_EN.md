The company's management also focuses on intermediating the purchase and sale of waste, following the trends of the Romanian market in this field, a market that is in constant development. We believe that recycling is one of the simplest methods to protect the planet from pollution.

## Services

- Installation and assembly: our automation specialists ensure the mounting, installation, and commissioning of purchased equipment, as well as technical inspections both during the warranty period and post-warranty.
