TG 1800

DESCRIPTION

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Single-shaft shredder with blade for large productions.

Used for primary shredding of various materials: copper and aluminum
cables, non-ferrous metals such as aluminum profiles, rubber and
plastics, wood.

Hydraulic rotation of the rotor with possibility of variation in speed
and therefore of the working force.

Hydraulic pusher, conveyor belt with magnetic separator, electric power
supply on board, mounted on hooklift.
